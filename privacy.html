Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how HumanOS ("HumanOS", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the HumanOS mobile application.

HumanOS is intended for general health, fitness, wellness, and personal tracking purposes. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or emergency services.

1. Information We Handle

HumanOS may handle information that you provide directly or authorize the app to access, including:

  • Account information, such as your name or email address.
  • Wellness information that you choose to enter, such as habits, routines, goals, notes, check-ins, and personal progress.
  • Health and fitness information that you authorize HumanOS to access through Apple Health and HealthKit.
  • Technical information needed to operate and secure the app, such as device type, operating system version, app version, and diagnostic or error information.

The specific information handled by HumanOS depends on the features you use and the permissions you choose to grant.

2. Apple Health and HealthKit

HumanOS may request permission to read information from or write information to Apple Health using Apple's HealthKit framework.

HealthKit access is optional and requires your explicit authorization. HumanOS only requests access to HealthKit data needed to provide the features you choose to use.

Depending on the features available in the app, this information may include health, activity, fitness, sleep, body measurement, workout, or other wellness-related information supported by HealthKit.

You can review, change, or revoke HumanOS access to HealthKit data at any time through the Apple Health app or your device's privacy settings.

HumanOS does not use HealthKit information for advertising, marketing, data mining, credit decisions, insurance decisions, or user tracking.

HumanOS does not sell HealthKit information or share it with data brokers, advertising networks, or advertising platforms.

3. How We Use Information

We may use information handled by HumanOS to:

  • Provide and operate the HumanOS application.
  • Authenticate users and protect their accounts.
  • Store and display information entered by users.
  • Read or write health and fitness information that you authorize through Apple Health and HealthKit.
  • Help users track habits, routines, goals, wellness information, and personal progress.
  • Generate app features, summaries, charts, trends, or insights based on information available to HumanOS.
  • Maintain, secure, troubleshoot, and improve the reliability of the app.
  • Respond to support requests and feedback.

4. Health and Wellness Disclaimer

HumanOS is intended for general wellness, fitness, and personal tracking purposes only.

HumanOS does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment recommendations, or emergency medical services.

Information displayed by HumanOS should not be used as a substitute for advice from a physician or other qualified healthcare professional.

You should consult a qualified healthcare professional before making decisions that may affect your health, medication, treatment, exercise routine, or nutrition.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

Information may be processed by service providers that help us host, maintain, secure, troubleshoot, or operate HumanOS. These providers may only process information as necessary to perform services on our behalf.

HealthKit information is not shared with third parties for advertising, marketing, data-broker, or user-tracking purposes.

We may disclose information when reasonably necessary to comply with applicable law, a valid legal process, or a governmental request, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users, HumanOS, or others.

6. Advertising and Tracking

HumanOS does not use personal, health, fitness, or wellness information for third-party advertising.

HumanOS does not track users across apps or websites owned by other companies for advertising or advertising measurement.

7. Data Storage and Security

Some information may be processed locally on your device. Other information may be stored using systems or service providers needed to operate HumanOS.

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, loss, or destruction.

However, no electronic transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide HumanOS, maintain security, respond to support requests, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

Information stored only on your device may be removed when you delete it from the app, remove the app, or erase the device, depending on how the feature operates.
